About the dataset

The IAU CRS Dataset is a collection of definitions of coordinate reference systems for solar bodies based on the IAU report published by the WG Cartographic Coordinates & Rotational Elements (WGCCRE). The representation of definitions of coordonate reference systems follows the WKT-CRS standard. The IAU CRS Dataset is maintained by the Planetary DWG and hosted by the PDSSD infrastucture.
